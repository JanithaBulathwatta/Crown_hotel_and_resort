About

Nestled amidst the verdant hills and cultural richness of Kandy, Sri Lanka, Hotel Crown stands as a beacon of hospitality and elegance. Overlooking breathtaking vistas of lush greenery and the majestic Knuckles Mountain Range, our hotel offers a serene retreat for travelers seeking a blend of luxury and tranquility. From the moment guests step into our grand lobby, they are enveloped in a world of refined comfort and gracious service. Our meticulously appointed rooms and suites provide a sanctuary of relaxation, each thoughtfully designed to capture the essence of contemporary luxury while paying homage to the local heritage and culture. Whether indulging in authentic Sri Lankan flavors at our signature restaurant, unwinding by the poolside amidst tropical gardens, or exploring the rich tapestry of history and spirituality that surrounds us, every moment at Hotel Crown is an invitation to discover the true essence of Kandy's charm. With our unwavering commitment to excellence and unparalleled hospitality, we ensure that every stay is an unforgettable experience, leaving guests with cherished memories of their time in this enchanting city.
